Elite Strength Program

8 Week Advanced Calisthenics Program

Weeks 1–2

Monday — Planche

Planche Lean 6 sets
Pseudo Push-ups 5x10

Tuesday — Pull

Weighted Pull-ups 5x5
Front Lever Raises 5x6

Thursday — Handstand

Handstand Push-ups 6x5
Wall Handstand Hold 5 sets

Weeks 3–4

Increase hold time and add reps

Weeks 5–6

Add weighted progressions where possible

Weeks 7–8

Focus on full skill attempts and max strength
