Elite Calisthenics Program

This level focuses on elite strength skills including planche, hefesto, maltese and front lever pullups.

Front lever
Handstand pushups

Elite Tips

Elite calisthenics requires extremely strong connective tissues. Never rush progressions.

Master perfect form before progressing to harder skills like maltese or hefesto.
