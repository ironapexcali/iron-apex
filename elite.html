Iron Apex Elite Program

This program targets elite calisthenics strength and advanced bodyweight skills such as straddle planche, front lever pullups, one arm pullups and V-sit compression.

Requirements

20+ pullups
Weighted pullup +25kg
Consistent clean muscle ups
Front lever progression
Advanced tuck planche
Freestanding handstand

Primary Skill Goals

• Straddle / full planche
• Front lever pullups
• One arm pullup progressions
• Freestanding handstand pushups
• 90° handstand pushups
• V-sit compression

Weekly Structure

Monday — Heavy Pull Strength
Tuesday — Planche + Handstand
Wednesday — Mobility / Recovery
Thursday — Push Strength
Friday — Skill + Core
Saturday — Explosive Strength
Sunday — Rest

Warmup (10 minutes)

Jump rope — 2 minutes Band shoulder rotations — 3 × 20 Scapula pullups — 3 × 12 Dead hang — 2 × 40 seconds Shoulder mobility drills — 4 minutes

Elite athletes should always warm up shoulders and elbows thoroughly.

MONDAY — Heavy Pull Strength

Weighted Pullups 6 × 4 Rest: 3 minutes
Front Lever Pullups 5 × 3
Archer Pullups 4 × 6 each side
Typewriter Pullups 4 × 5
Front Lever Hold 6 × 12 seconds
Toes to Bar 5 × 12

TUESDAY — Planche + Handstand

Planche Leans 5 × 20 seconds
Advanced Tuck Planche 5 × 15 seconds
Straddle Planche Attempts 6 attempts
Freestanding Handstand Practice 15 minutes
Handstand Pushups (freestanding or wall) 5 × 6
Wide Handstand Hold 4 × 25 seconds

Maintain shoulder protraction during planche work.

WEDNESDAY — Mobility

Deep squat hold — 3 minutes
Shoulder extension stretch — 2 minutes
Hamstring stretch — 2 minutes
Bridge hold — 3 × 30 seconds
Thoracic spine mobility — 3 minutes

THURSDAY — Push Strength

Weighted Dips 5 × 6
Handstand Pushups 6 × 6
90 Degree Handstand Hold 5 × 10 seconds
Planche Pushups 5 × 4
Pseudo Planche Pushups 5 × 10
Plank 4 × 1 minute

FRIDAY — Skill + Core

Muscle Ups 6 × 3
Front Lever Practice 10 minutes
V-Sit Progression 5 × 15 seconds
Compression Raises 4 × 15
Dragon Flags 5 × 6
Hanging Leg Raises 4 × 15

Compression strength is essential for V-sit development.

SATURDAY — Explosive Strength

Explosive Pullups 6 × 4
Weighted Muscle Ups 5 × 3
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6
Assisted One Arm Pullups 5 × 3 each arm
Dead Hang 3 × 60 seconds
Dragon Flags 4 × 8

Long Term Skill Targets

Athletes training consistently should progress toward:

Straddle planche
Front lever pullups
One arm pullup
Freestanding handstand pushups
V-sit

Recovery Advice

Sleep 7–9 hours per night.

Eat sufficient calories and protein.

Reduce training volume if elbows or shoulders become irritated.

Consistency over months builds elite calisthenics strength.
