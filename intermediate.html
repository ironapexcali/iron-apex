Iron Apex Intermediate Program

This program is designed for athletes who already have a solid strength base and want to begin mastering explosive calisthenics skills and inverted strength.

Requirements

8 pullups
10 dips
30 pushups

Primary Goals

• Clean muscle ups
• Explosive pulling strength
• Front lever progressions
• Handstand control
• Handstand pushup strength
• Core strength for V-sit

Weekly Schedule

Monday — Pull Strength
Tuesday — Skill + Handstand
Wednesday — Mobility
Thursday — Push Strength
Friday — Skill + Core
Saturday — Pull Strength
Sunday — Rest

Warmup (10 minutes)

Jump rope — 2 minutes Arm circles — 2 × 20 Scapula pullups — 3 × 10 Shoulder mobility — 3 minutes Dead hang — 2 × 30 seconds

Shoulder preparation is essential before explosive pulling and handstand work.

MONDAY — Pull Strength

Pullups 5 × 8 Rest: 2 minutes
Weighted Pullups 4 × 5
High Pullups (chest to bar) 5 × 4
Front Lever Tuck Hold 5 × 12 seconds
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6
Hanging Leg Raises 4 × 12

Pull explosively and aim to bring the bar to your lower chest.

TUESDAY — Skill + Handstand

Muscle Up Band Practice 8 attempts
Jumping Muscle Ups 6 attempts
Pullover Practice 5 attempts
Handstand Practice 15 minutes
Wall Handstand Hold 4 × 30 seconds
Crow Pose 5 × 30 seconds

Spend time learning balance in the handstand rather than rushing reps.

WEDNESDAY — Mobility Day

Deep squat hold — 3 minutes
Shoulder extension stretch — 2 minutes
Hamstring stretch — 2 minutes
Bridge hold — 3 × 20 seconds
Thoracic mobility drills — 3 minutes

THURSDAY — Push Strength

Dips 5 × 10
Weighted Dips 4 × 6
Pike Pushups 5 × 10
Wall Handstand Pushups 5 × 5
Pseudo Planche Pushups 4 × 8
Plank 4 × 1 minute

Pike pushups and wall HSPU develop the strength needed for freestanding HSPU later.

FRIDAY — Skill + Core

Muscle Up Attempts 6 attempts
Swing Practice (Bar Control) 10 minutes
Skin The Cat 4 × 5
L-Sit Hold 5 × 20 seconds
Compression Raises 4 × 12
Hanging Knee Raises 4 × 15

Compression strength is essential for progressing toward a V-sit.

SATURDAY — Pull Strength

Weighted Pullups 5 × 5
Explosive Pullups 5 × 4
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6
Archer Pullups 4 × 5 each side
Dead Hang 3 × 45 seconds
Dragon Flags 4 × 6

Progression Goals

Before progressing to the Advanced program you should reach:

15 pullups
20 dips
Clean muscle up
10 second tuck front lever
5 wall handstand pushups

Recovery Advice

Sleep 7–9 hours per night.

Eat enough protein and calories to recover.

Consistency is the most important factor in calisthenics progress.
