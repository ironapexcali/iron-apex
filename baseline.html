IRON APEX BASELINE

The Baseline Protocol builds the raw strength required before progressing into real calisthenics skill training.

Most beginners fail because they attempt advanced skills without developing the pulling strength and core control needed first. This program fixes that by focusing on foundational strength.

Program Overview

Duration: 8 weeks
Training frequency: 5 sessions per week
Session length: 35–60 minutes

The goal of this phase is to develop the minimum strength standards required before entering the Intermediate Iron Apex programs.

By the end of the Baseline Protocol you should have developed the strength foundation required for future skill training such as muscle-ups, handstands and front lever progressions.

Equipment Required

Pull-up Bar

Used for hangs, pull-up progressions and core exercises.

Dip Bars

Required for dip support holds and pressing strength.

Resistance Band

Optional assistance for pull-up training.

Training Rules

Strict Reps

Every repetition should be controlled with full range of motion. Avoid swinging or half repetitions.

Controlled Tempo

Lowering phases should be slow and controlled to build strength and improve movement mechanics.

Stop Before Failure

Leave one or two reps in reserve so technique stays clean throughout the workout.

Weekly Training Schedule

Monday — Pull + Core

Dead Hang — 3 sets max time build grip strength and shoulder stability
Negative Pull-ups — 4 x 3–5 reps 5 second controlled descent
Australian Rows — 3 x 10–12 reps pull chest to bar
Tuck L-Sit — 4 x 10–15 seconds develop core compression

Tuesday — Push Strength

Incline Push-ups — 4 x 10–12 reps keep body straight
Negative Dips — 4 x 3–5 reps slow 5 second descent
Scapular Push-ups — 3 x 10 reps shoulder stability
Plank Hold — 3 x 45 seconds core stability

Wednesday — Active Recovery

20 minute walk
Light stretching and mobility

Thursday — Pull Strength

Band Assisted Pull-ups — 4 x 6 reps
Scapular Pull-ups — 4 x 10 reps
Hanging Knee Raises — 3 x 10 reps
Passive Hang — 3 sets max

Friday — Push + Core

Strict Push-ups — 4 sets max reps
Bench Dips — 3 x 12 reps
L-Sit Attempts — 4 x 8 seconds
Hollow Body Hold — 3 x 30 seconds

Saturday — Full Body

Australian Rows — 4 x 12 reps
Push-ups — 4 x 10 reps
Dip Support Hold — 3 x 30 seconds
Knee Raises — 3 x 10 reps

Sunday — Rest

Full recovery day
