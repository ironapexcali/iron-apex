Advanced Calisthenics Program

At this stage you will start mastering real calisthenics skills like the muscle-up, front lever and handstand pushups.

Requirements

15 pullups
20 dips
Freestanding handstand

Weekly Schedule

Monday — Pull

  • Weighted pullups – 5x5
  • Muscle-up practice – 10 sets
  • Front lever pulls – 4x5
  • Front lever holds – 6 attempts

Tuesday — Push

  • Weighted dips – 5x5
  • Handstand pushups – 5 sets
  • Pushup variations – 4x20
  • Planche lean – 5 sets

Wednesday — Rest

Thursday — Skill Day

  • Muscle-up practice
  • Front lever practice
  • Back lever progression
  • Skin the cat

Friday — Strength

  • Weighted pullups
  • Weighted dips
  • Handstand pushups
  • Advanced tuck planche
  • 90° hold practice

Saturday — Mobility

  • Shoulder mobility
  • Bridge
  • Compression drills
  • Pancake stretch

Sunday — Rest

Progression Tips

Muscle-ups require explosive pulling strength. Focus on pulling the bar to your chest.

Front lever progressions should be trained consistently with strict form.
