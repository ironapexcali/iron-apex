Iron Apex Advanced Program

This program develops serious calisthenics strength. You will begin training toward elite skills such as front lever, advanced tuck planche, strong handstand pushups and explosive muscle ups.

Requirements

Clean muscle up
15+ pullups
20 dips
10 second tuck front lever

Primary Goals

• Strong weighted pullups
• Consistent clean muscle ups
• Front lever progression
• Advanced tuck planche
• Handstand pushups
• Core strength for V-sit

Weekly Structure

Monday — Heavy Pull Strength
Tuesday — Skill Training
Wednesday — Mobility / Recovery
Thursday — Push Strength
Friday — Skill + Core
Saturday — Pull Strength + Explosive Work
Sunday — Rest

Warmup (10 minutes)

Jump rope — 2 minutes Band shoulder rotations — 3 × 20 Scapula pullups — 3 × 10 Dead hang — 2 × 30 sec Shoulder mobility drills — 3 minutes

At advanced levels, protecting the shoulders is critical. Never rush your warmup.

MONDAY — Heavy Pull Strength

Weighted Pullups 5 sets × 5 reps Rest: 3 minutes
High Pullups (chest to bar) 5 sets × 4 reps
Front Lever Tuck Hold 5 × 15 seconds
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6 reps
Archer Pullups 4 × 5 each side
Hanging Leg Raises 4 × 15

Explosive pulling strength is essential for clean muscle ups and advanced front lever training.

TUESDAY — Skill Training

Muscle Ups 6 sets × 3 reps
Explosive Pullups 5 × 4
Front Lever Practice 10 minutes
Handstand Balance 15 minutes
Skin The Cat 4 × 5 reps
Crow Pose / Balance 5 × 30 seconds

Muscle ups should be clean with minimal kip. Focus on smooth bar transition.

WEDNESDAY — Mobility Day

Deep squat hold 3 minutes
Shoulder extension stretch 2 minutes
Hamstring stretch 2 minutes
Bridge hold 3 × 30 seconds
Thoracic mobility drills 3 minutes

THURSDAY — Push Strength

Weighted Dips 5 × 6
Handstand Pushups (wall) 5 × 6
Pseudo Planche Pushups 5 × 10
Advanced Tuck Planche 5 × 10 seconds
Pike Pushups 4 × 10
Plank 4 × 1 minute

Leaning forward during pseudo planche pushups helps build the shoulder strength required for planche.

FRIDAY — Skill + Core

Front Lever Practice 10 minutes
Muscle Up Attempts 5 attempts
L-Sit Hold 5 × 25 seconds
Compression Raises 4 × 12
Dragon Flags 4 × 6
Hanging Toes to Bar 4 × 10

Compression strength is key for developing the V-sit later.

SATURDAY — Pull Strength + Explosive Work

Weighted Pullups 5 × 5
Explosive Pullups 5 × 4
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6
Archer Pullups 4 × 5 each side
Dead Hang 3 × 60 seconds
Dragon Flags 4 × 6

Progression Targets

Before progressing to the Elite program you should reach:

20 pullups
Weighted pullup +25kg
Consistent muscle ups
15 second front lever progression
15 second advanced tuck planche

Recovery Advice

Advanced training requires excellent recovery.

Sleep at least 7–9 hours per night.

Eat enough protein and calories.

If your elbows or shoulders feel strained, reduce volume temporarily.
