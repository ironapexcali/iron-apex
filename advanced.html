Iron Apex Advanced Program

This program is designed for athletes who already possess strong calisthenics fundamentals and are working toward advanced strength skills such as front lever and planche.

Requirements

Clean muscle up
15+ pullups
20 dips
10 second tuck front lever
5 wall handstand pushups

Primary Goals

• Strong weighted pullups
• Consistent muscle ups
• Front lever progression
• Advanced tuck planche
• Freestanding handstand
• Handstand pushups
• Core strength for V-sit

Weekly Schedule

Monday — Pull Strength
Tuesday — Handstand + Skill
Wednesday — Mobility / Recovery
Thursday — Push Strength
Friday — Skill + Core
Saturday — Explosive Strength
Sunday — Rest

Warmup (10 minutes)

Jump rope — 2 minutes Band shoulder rotations — 3 × 20 Scapula pullups — 3 × 10 Dead hang — 2 × 30 seconds Shoulder mobility drills — 3 minutes

Proper shoulder preparation prevents elbow and shoulder injuries.

MONDAY — Pull Strength

Weighted Pullups 5 × 5 Rest: 3 minutes
High Pullups (chest to bar) 5 × 4
Front Lever Tuck Hold 5 × 15 seconds
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6
Archer Pullups 4 × 5 each side
Hanging Toes to Bar 4 × 12

High pullups develop the explosive strength required for strict muscle ups.

TUESDAY — Handstand + Skill

Freestanding Handstand Practice 15 minutes
Wall Handstand Pushups 5 × 6
Muscle Ups 6 × 3
Skin The Cat 4 × 5
Crow Pose 5 × 30 seconds
Handstand Balance Drills 10 minutes

Spend time controlling balance rather than rushing reps.

WEDNESDAY — Mobility Day

Deep squat hold — 3 minutes
Shoulder extension stretch — 2 minutes
Hamstring stretch — 2 minutes
Bridge hold — 3 × 30 seconds
Thoracic mobility drills — 3 minutes

THURSDAY — Push Strength

Weighted Dips 5 × 6
Handstand Pushups 5 × 6
Pseudo Planche Pushups 5 × 10
Advanced Tuck Planche 5 × 12 seconds
Pike Pushups 4 × 10
Plank 4 × 1 minute

Lean forward during pseudo planche pushups to strengthen the shoulders.

FRIDAY — Skill + Core

Front Lever Practice 10 minutes
Muscle Up Attempts 6 attempts
L-Sit Hold 5 × 25 seconds
Compression Raises 4 × 12
Dragon Flags 4 × 6
Hanging Leg Raises 4 × 15

Compression strength helps build toward a full V-sit.

SATURDAY — Explosive Strength

Explosive Pullups 5 × 4
Weighted Pullups 5 × 5
Front Lever Rows 4 × 6
Archer Pullups 4 × 5 each side
Dead Hang 3 × 60 seconds
Dragon Flags 4 × 8

Progression Goals

Before moving to the Elite program you should reach:

20 pullups
Weighted pullup +25kg
Consistent muscle ups
15 second front lever progression
15 second advanced tuck planche
5 freestanding handstand pushups

Recovery Advice

Sleep at least 7–9 hours per night.

Eat sufficient protein and calories.

Avoid excessive training to failure.

Consistency is the key to mastering advanced calisthenics skills.
