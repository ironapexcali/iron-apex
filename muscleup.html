THE "C" SWING

You cannot pull straight up. You must swing slightly forward to create a 'C' path, allowing your body to clear the bar during the transition.

FALSE GRIP

Roll your wrists over the bar. This shortens the distance of the turn-over and prevents the "stuck" position at the chest.

MASTER THE MOMENTUM

The biggest mistake is a dead-hang pull. You must learn to use a weighted-body swing. As you swing forward, engage your lats to "pull" the bar down to your hips. Momentum isn't cheating—it's mechanics.
