My Story

When I first started training I was weak, lacked mobility and constantly hit plateaus. Progress felt slow and most training advice online didn't really explain how to build real calisthenics strength.

Instead of chasing skills too early, I focused on building real pulling strength, pushing strength and mobility. Once the fundamentals improved everything else became easier.

Within about six months of consistent calisthenics training my strength and physique started changing dramatically. Pull-ups improved, skills became easier and training finally felt structured.

Iron Apex was created to share that structured approach so others can progress faster without hitting the same plateaus.
