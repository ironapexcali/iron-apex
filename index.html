EVOLUTION
THE PROCESS

THE 1.5 YEAR BLUEPRINT

This is not an overnight hack. This is a forged timeline of mechanical adaptation. Follow the map.

MONTH 01 - 03

THE AWAKENING

We start from scratch. This phase is all about building a bulletproof foundation. We'll focus heavy on scapular strength, dead hangs, and controlled negatives so your body gets used to the load.

MONTH 04 - 06

THE FORGE

This is where the magic happens. You'll unlock strict, clean reps—hitting that 10 pull-up and 15 dip milestone. We'll also start basic pike push-ups for your shoulders and build a rock-solid core with L-sits.

MONTH 07 - 10

THE CATALYST

Time for your first muscle-up. We'll also introduce wall handstand push-ups and start strapping weight to your pull-ups (+10kg to +20kg). This is when you really start seeing serious muscle growth.

MONTH 11 - 18

IRON APEX

Now we're in the elite territory. We shift focus to advanced static holds like the Front Lever, 90-degree HSPU, and balancing a freestanding handstand. One-arm pull-up training begins here.

SYSTEM FAQ

Do I need to go to a gym for this?
Nope. My entire system is built around using your own bodyweight. As long as you have a pull-up bar, some dip bars, and a little floor space, you're good to go.
How many days a week do I train?
Most of the programs are 4 to 5 days a week. I highly recommend taking your rest days seriously—your muscles need time to recover and grow.
I can't do a pull-up yet. Where do I start?
Start with the Beginner Protocol. It is specifically designed to build your foundational core stability and vertical pulling strength from zero up to your first 5 strict reps.
Do you offer refunds?
Since these are digital training guides, I don't offer refunds. I give you the exact steps to succeed, but you have to be the one to put in the work.
