THE PROCESS

THE 1.5 YEAR BLUEPRINT

This is not an overnight hack. This is a forged timeline of mechanical adaptation. Follow the map.

MONTH 01 - 03

THE AWAKENING

Starting from zero. Building the absolute baseline of connective tissue strength. Focusing heavily on scapular retraction, dead hangs, and negative pull-ups. The nervous system begins to adapt to your own bodyweight.

MONTH 04 - 06

THE FORGE

Strict reps unlock. Hitting the 10 clean pull-up mark and 15 strict dips. Basic pike push-ups begin setting the foundation for handstands. The core solidifies with L-sits, preparing the foundation for dynamic transitions.

MONTH 07 - 10

THE CATALYST

The first muscle-up is achieved. Wall handstand push-ups (HSPU) are introduced. Weight is added to pull-ups (+10kg to +20kg) to force hyper-adaptation. Shoulders broaden, lats flare.

MONTH 11 - 18

IRON APEX

Elite mastery. Advanced statics like the Front Lever, 90-Degree HSPU, and freestanding balance become the primary focus. One-arm pull-up progressions start. You are engineering power.

SYSTEM FAQ

Do I need to go to a gym for this?
No. The entire Iron Apex system is built around gravity and leverage. As long as you have access to a pull-up bar, dip bars, and floor space, you can execute 100% of these protocols.
How many days a week do I train?
Most protocols are 4 to 5 days a week. We strictly enforce rest days. Neurological adaptation requires deep rest.
I can't do a pull-up yet. Where do I start?
Start with the Beginner Protocol. It is specifically designed to build your foundational core stability and vertical pulling strength from zero up to your first 5 strict reps.
