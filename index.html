My Story

When I started calisthenics I couldn't do a single pull-up.

I lacked mobility and constantly hit plateaus.

Through structured training I eventually progressed to 30 pull-ups, +50kg weighted pull-ups, clean slow muscle-ups, front lever, handstand pushups and straddle planche.

Iron Apex was built to help others reach that level without wasting years on bad training methods.
