My Story

When I first started training I was weak, lacked mobility and constantly hit plateaus. Progress felt slow and most training advice online didn't really explain how to build real calisthenics strength.

Over time I started focusing on the fundamentals — pulling strength, pushing strength, core control and mobility. Instead of chasing skills too early I built a solid strength base first.

Within about six months of consistent calisthenics training my physique and strength started to change dramatically. Pull-ups improved, skills became easier and training finally started to feel structured.

Iron Apex was created to share that same structured approach so others can progress faster without hitting the same plateaus.
