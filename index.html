Who’s behind Iron Apex

Iron Apex was built around one goal: developing real calisthenics strength. Pull-ups, muscle-ups, explosive power and total body control.

Everything here focuses on simple training principles that actually build strength instead of complicated routines that go nowhere.

Why Iron Apex exists

Most workout programs are either overly complicated or completely generic. I wanted something different: a system focused on real calisthenics strength.

Iron Apex focuses on pulling power, explosive muscle-ups, planche strength and control on the bar.

Iron Apex Athletes

Iron Apex is about people chasing elite bodyweight strength — mastering pull-ups, muscle-ups and full control of their body.

If you love calisthenics and want to get brutally strong, you’re in the right place.
