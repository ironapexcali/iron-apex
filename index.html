Iron Apex Training System

Build elite calisthenics strength.

Iron Apex focuses on real bodyweight strength training — pull-ups, muscle-ups, front lever, planche and total body control.

The programs below are designed to guide athletes from beginner strength all the way to advanced calisthenics skills through structured weekly workouts and progressive training.

Why Iron Apex Exists

Most workout programs are either overly complicated or completely generic. Iron Apex focuses on simple training principles that build real calisthenics strength through consistent work and progressive training.

The goal is simple: develop elite bodyweight strength through pull-ups, muscle-ups, levers, planche training and strong fundamental movement patterns.
