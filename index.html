My Journey

When I first started calisthenics I struggled with mobility, poor technique and very slow progress. Like many beginners I rushed skills too early and constantly ran into plateaus. I lacked the understanding of how to build strength properly and how mobility affects performance. Over time I rebuilt my training from the ground up. I focused on fundamentals, structured progression and mobility. These programs are built from that experience and designed to help athletes avoid the mistakes that cause most plateaus.
