My Story

When I first started training I was weak, lacked mobility and constantly hit plateaus.

Instead of chasing skills too early I focused on building real pulling strength, pushing strength and mobility.

Within about six months of consistent calisthenics training my strength and physique started changing dramatically.

Iron Apex was created to share that structured approach so others can progress faster without hitting the same plateaus.
