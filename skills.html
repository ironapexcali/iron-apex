Intermediate Calisthenics Program

At this level you start building real calisthenics strength. You will begin training weighted exercises and learning your first advanced skills like front lever and muscle-up progressions.

Requirements

8 pullups
10 dips
30 pushups

Session Structure (~1h30)

  • Warm-up – 15 min
  • Skill training – 20 min
  • Strength training – 40 min
  • Core training – 10 min
  • Mobility – 10 min

Warm-Up Routine

  • Jump rope – 3 min
  • Shoulder mobility – 2 min
  • Wrist prep – 2 min
  • Scapula pullups – 2x10
  • Pushups – 2x15

Weekly Schedule

Monday — Weighted Pull

  • Weighted pullups – 5x5
  • High pullups – 4x5
  • Chinups – 4x8
  • Front lever tuck holds – 6 attempts
  • Hanging leg raises – 4x12

Tuesday — Push Strength

  • Weighted dips – 5x5
  • Pike pushups – 4x10
  • Pushup variations – 4x20
  • L-sit holds – 6 attempts
  • V-sit progression

Wednesday — Rest

Thursday — Skill Training

  • Bar swings – 10 min
  • Pullover progression – 10 min
  • Skin the cat – 5 sets
  • Banded muscle-ups – 6 sets
  • Front lever progression – 10 min

Friday — Strength

  • Weighted pullups – 4x6
  • Weighted dips – 4x6
  • Pushup variations – 4x20
  • Tuck planche practice – 10 min

Saturday — Mobility

  • Shoulder mobility routine
  • Compression drills
  • Bridge practice
  • Pancake stretch

Sunday — Rest

Progression Tips

Focus on explosive pullups. High pullups are the foundation for learning the muscle-up.

Once you can perform explosive pullups and clean weighted pullups you will be ready for the Advanced program.
