Beginner Calisthenics Program

Session length ~90 minutes. Build strength, technique and bar control.

Warm Up (15 min)
Dead Hang 3x30s
Scapula Pull Ups 3x12
Band Rows 3x20
Strength (30 min)
Pull Ups 5x5
Australian Rows 4x12
Negative Pull Ups 4x5
Freestyle Basics (20 min)
Basic Bar Swing Practice 10 min
Grip Switching Practice
Core (15 min)
Hanging Knee Raises 4x12
Plank 3x60s
Stretching
Lat Stretch
Hamstring Stretch
