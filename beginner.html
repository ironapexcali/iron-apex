Iron Apex Beginner Program

Goal: Build your foundation for serious calisthenics strength.

This program prepares your body for: clean pullups, dips, explosive pulling, core strength and eventually skills like muscle ups, front lever and planche.

Requirements

0–5 pullups
0–5 dips
0–20 pushups

Training Philosophy

Beginners should not chase advanced skills immediately. Instead we focus on strength fundamentals.

Strong basics create fast progress later.

Your focus:

• Perfect form
• Strong pullups
• Core control
• Consistency

Weekly Schedule

Monday — Pull Strength
Tuesday — Push Strength
Wednesday — Mobility Day
Thursday — Pull Strength
Friday — Push + Core
Saturday — Mixed Strength
Sunday — Rest

Warmup Routine (10 minutes)

Jump rope — 2 minutes Arm circles — 2x20 Scapula pullups — 3x8 Shoulder mobility circles — 3 minutes Dead hang — 2x30 sec

Tip: Never skip warmups. Shoulder injuries are extremely common in beginners.

MONDAY — Pull Strength (90 minutes)

Assisted Pullups 5 sets × 5 reps Rest: 2 min
Negative Pullups 4 sets × 4 reps Slow 5 second descent
Inverted Rows 4 sets × 10 reps
Dead Hang 4 × 30 sec
Hanging Knee Raises 4 × 12

Focus on pulling your chest to the bar instead of just getting your chin over.

TUESDAY — Push Strength

Pushups 5 × 12 Rest: 90 sec
Bench Dips 4 × 10
Pike Pushups 4 × 8
Wall Handstand Practice 10 minutes
Plank 4 × 45 sec

Keep your core tight during pushups to avoid lower back arching.

WEDNESDAY — Mobility Day

Deep squat hold 3 minutes
Shoulder stretch 2 minutes
Hamstring stretch 2 minutes
Bridge hold 3 × 20 sec
Thoracic spine mobility 3 minutes

THURSDAY — Pull Strength

Pullups 5 × max reps
Assisted Pullups 4 × 6
Australian Rows 4 × 12
Dead Hang 4 × 30 sec
Hanging Knee Raises 4 × 12

FRIDAY — Push + Core

Pushups 5 × 15
Bench Dips 4 × 12
Pike Pushups 4 × 8
L Sit (tuck) 5 × 15 sec
Plank 4 × 1 minute

SATURDAY — Mixed Strength

Pullups 5 × max
Pushups 5 × 20
Squats 4 × 20
Hanging Knee Raises 4 × 12
Crow Pose 5 attempts × 20 sec

Balance training develops coordination and prepares you for planche training later.

Progression Rules

Increase reps when exercises become easy.

Once you reach:

10 pullups
15 dips
40 pushups

You are ready for the Intermediate program.

Recovery Advice

Sleep 7–9 hours.

Eat enough protein.

Avoid training to complete failure every set.

Consistency beats intensity.
