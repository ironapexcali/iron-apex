Beginner Calisthenics Program

This program builds the fundamentals needed for advanced calisthenics. Focus on good form and consistent progression.

Workout A

Pull-ups — 4 x 5
Push-ups — 4 x 12
Dips — 4 x 8
Plank — 3 x 45 seconds

Workout B

Rows — 4 x 10
Pike Push-ups — 4 x 10
Squats — 4 x 15
Leg Raises — 3 x 12
